# PSI canonical note: Racio

Status: paraphrased canonical note for REI-v3. Do not treat this as a verbatim transcript.

Source: `Racio.pdf`, pages 1-2.

## Core definition

Racio is the youngest REI processor. It is conscious, verbal, numerical, analytical, sequential, planning-oriented, structured, and explicit. It is the processor that handles words and numbers, and the one we directly experience as conscious thought.

## Canonical traits

- Youngest processor.
- Thinks analytically and searches for causes and relations.
- Plans, predicts, structures, compares data, and makes things orderly.
- Seeks paths toward goals.
- Uses words and numbers, including silent inner speech.
- Translates the language of Emocio and Instinkt.
- Can also pretend to speak in their name, which makes it hard to identify.
- Enables self-awareness by controlling logical conclusion, conscious deliberation, and speech.
- Is not the whole consciousness or the whole self; that is a mistaken impression caused by direct awareness of Racio.
- Can be diplomatic, useful, structured, and effective.
- Can also be greedy, cold, calculating, self-serving, and rationalizing.

## LLM contract consequence

Racio output must be dry, structured, explicit, and must separate known facts from unknowns. It must always include rationalization risk, because Racio can verbalize a conclusion produced by Emocio or Instinkt and then present it as its own reason.
