# PSI canonical note: Emocio

Status: paraphrased canonical note for REI-v3. Do not treat this as a verbatim transcript.

Source: `Emocio.pdf`, pages 1-3.

## Core definition

Emocio is the image-based and desire-based processor. It links images by feeling, lives through scenes, bodies, faces, movement, beauty, status, group belonging, recognition, competition, sexuality, improvisation, and immediate experience. It is not merely "emotion" and not generic empathy.

## Canonical traits

- Opposite of Instinkt.
- Believes good can prevail over bad and that things can still work out.
- Thinks in images and links them by feeling.
- Simplifies and improvises.
- Is open, visible, social, group-identifying, attention-seeking, and competitive.
- Its defense is attack.
- When desired images become real, it laughs; when they break, it rages.
- Has a strong relation to movement, sport, body expression, beauty, touch, performance, and sexuality.
- Can be generous and broad, but also spendthrift, unreliable, impulsive, and hooked on substitute solutions.
- It changes images slowly and lets go of broken images with difficulty.

## LLM contract consequence

Emocio output must not become generic empathy. It must be a Racio-translation of a visual/social/desire/status signal: current image, desired image, broken image, recognition need, pride/shame, rivalry, body expression, and attack pressure.
