For a Racio packet, `benefit` must be:

- explicit;
- attributable to a named beneficiary;
- grounded in an explicit goal or material/strategic consequence.

not Racio benefit. A helping action can still be selected through Racio when the
packet identifies the benefit and its consequence chain.

## Required case-specific consequences

### Loan Instinkt route

The frozen critique is strengthened. A future packet must expose both sides of
the native tension:

- care, compassion, genuine need, willingness to sacrifice, attachment, and
  fear of losing the person;
- own scarcity, repayment trust, boundary, possible loss, and money/relationship
  recoverability.

Neither side is an expected answer.

### Public-credit Emocio route

The frozen `balanced positions` contamination finding is strengthened. Direct
source supports self-centered recognition, admiration, competition, attack
capacity, and special-rights justice. The route should show who is centered,
who observes, what recognition changes, and whether the obstacle remains.

The requirement for destructive public defeat is weakened: core-route mode
must not manufacture non-acceptance intensity. Public confrontation is not
hardcoded to win.

### Trip Instinkt route

The critique is partially revised. Home, unfamiliarity, distrust, danger,
attachment, and escape are direct-source core signals, not merely speculative
implementation fields. But the source does not prescribe staying home. A
future contrast must vary companion trust, lodging/transport verification, and
return path while keeping price, attraction, strategic benefit, rarity, and
refundability fixed.

### Loan Racio route

The critique is strengthened. Direct source establishes that helping another
person requires recognized benefit for Racio. A future packet must name the
beneficiary and explicit goal or consequence. Genuine need remains a source
fact for Instinkt care and a consequence for the borrower; it is not by itself
Racio utility.

## Prospective contract boundary

For future route-isolation work:

- envy is Instinkt core; only its strong expression is non-acceptance
  intensification;
- compassion, care, sacrifice, close-person protection, and the
  more-to-less justice representation are Instinkt core;
- help is Racio-native only through explicit, beneficiary-attributed,
  goal-grounded benefit;
- self-centered image, attention, admiration, competition, attack capacity,
  self-directed justice, and special rights are Emocio core;
- compulsive confirmation, excessive aggression, destructive public defeat,
  and inflated superiority remain outside core-route mode.

No score, expected option, runtime change, replay, or model execution follows
from these corrections.
